    RAG AI is an intelligent, retrieval-augmented system that ingests content from uploaded PDFs or website links and delivers accurate, context-aware answers based on user queries. It combines document-based search with generative AI capabilities to ensure high relevance and precision in responses. Users can switch to a general AI chat mode for open-ended conversations, similar to ChatGPT functionality. Designed for flexibility and efficiency, RAG AI enhances knowledge access and delivers dynamic, real-time insights.

  • Machine Learning

    Handwritten Digit Recognition

    The Handwritten Digit Recognition System is an AI-based application that identifies and classifies handwritten digits from images in real time. It is trained on the MNIST dataset and leverages deep learning models, particularly Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), for high accuracy. The system is capable of recognizing single and multi-digit sequences with robustness to varying handwriting styles. It processes 28x28 grayscale images and delivers fast, reliable results even in the presence of noise or distortion. Designed to automate manual recognition tasks, the system significantly reduces human effort and error in digit classification.
